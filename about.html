About ACM-XIM Chapter

The ACM-XIM Chapter is a distinguished technical student chapter at XIM University, renowned for its dedication to fostering a passion for technology among young minds. As a beacon of innovation and learning, the chapter serves as an inspiring hub where students can explore cutting-edge domains like Web Development, Internet of Things (IoT), Artificial Intelligence (AI), and Machine Learning (ML).

Through meticulously planned events and hands-on workshops, ACM-XIM not only imparts technical knowledge but also nurtures a culture of creativity and collaboration. Join us to unlock your potential and shape the future of technology!
